About me

My name is Bibek Bharati, and I am a student pursuing a Bachelor of IT in Cyber Security and Ethical Hacking from Coventry University at College Softwarica in Nepal. As an aspiring cybersecurity professional, I am passionate about using my skills to protect organizations and individuals from digital threats. Throughout my studies, I have gained knowledge and expertise in areas such as network security, information security, and ethical hacking. I have also participated in various cybersecurity competitions, including CTF (Capture The Flag) events, to hone my practical skills and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in the field. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also committed to giving back to my community. I am involved in various volunteer projects, including teaching basic computer skills to underprivileged children in my local area. As I move forward in my career, I am excited to continue learning and growing in the field of cybersecurity. Whether working independently or as part of a team, I am dedicated to using my skills to make a positive impact and help create a safer digital world.
